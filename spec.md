# epistemic — spec (design of record)

Changes only by deliberate decision. When a "maybe" in *Future direction* becomes real, it graduates out of this file into a Milestone with Issues.

## Goal

For an open question, identify the *crux* — the sub-question whose resolution most changes the answer — and surface promising research that may imminently resolve that crux. Deliverable is a prototype tool for the Future of Life Foundation "Epistemic Case Study Competition."
